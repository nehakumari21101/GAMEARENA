Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a tournament?

You can register for any tournament by visiting the Esports page, selecting the tournament you're interested in, and clicking the "Register Now" button. You'll need to create an account first if you don't have one.

What are the requirements to participate?

Requirements vary by tournament but generally include having the game installed, meeting any rank/level requirements, and being available during the scheduled tournament times. Specific requirements are listed on each tournament page.

How are prizes distributed?

Prizes are typically distributed within 30 days of tournament completion. Winners will be contacted via email with instructions on how to claim their prizes. Payment methods vary but often include bank transfer, PayPal, or in-game currency.

Can I form my own team?

Yes! Most tournaments allow you to register with your own team. You can create a team through your account dashboard and invite other players to join. Some tournaments also have solo registration options where you'll be matched with teammates.
